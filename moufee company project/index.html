About Our Company

FIRST OPTION CONTRACTING L.L.C

First option contracting, based in Chad, specializes in civil engineering, construction, and equipment services. We are committed to Chad’s growth, delivering top-tier services in road infrastructure, architecture, hydraulics, and hospital equipment. We’ve completed numerous projects for local and international clients, focusing on quality, innovation, and sustainability. Our deep community roots and local knowledge uniquely equip us to meet national project needs.

Our Service

  • Construction

    We offer a comprehensive range of construction services, from residential to public works. Our skilled team ensures timely, cost-effective delivery, managing every aspect, and without compromising on quality.

  • Civil Engineering

    Our civil engineering expertise covers road construction, bridges, water systems, and more. We use the latest engineering practices and technologies to ensure every project is structurally sound and efficiently executed.

  • Road Infrastructure

    We specialize in the development of road networks that are essential for connecting communities. From highways to local roads, our expertise ensures that every project is built to the highest standards.

  • Architecture

    Our architecture and building services span residential, commercial, and industrial projects. We prioritize creating structures that are functional, sustainable, and ensuring a wide range of construction.

  • Hospital Equipment

    We recognize the vital role of healthcare infrastructure in community well-being. Thus, we provide comprehensive services for supplying, delivering, and maintaining hospital equipment, and international-standard equipment.

  • Project Management

    We manage all aspects of construction projects, ensuring seamless execution and early issue resolution. Our structured approach guarantees first-rate delivery, and maintaining high standards throughout every phase.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead the way in infrastructure development across Chad by providing exceptional civil engineering solutions, construction services, and high-quality equipment. We strive to support national growth and progress by delivering projects that enhance the quality of life for our communities and contribute to the long-term prosperity of our country.

Our Vision

We aim to become a world-class regional leader in the provision of civil engineering, construction, and equipment, whereby our services are efficient, affordable, sustainable, and cost-effective services without compromising standards, quality and safety.

Why Choose Us

  • Local Expertise: Our local presence allows us to provide customized solutions perfectly aligned with the needs of our clients and communities.
  • Sustainable Practices: We promote sustainable practices in all our operations, safeguarding the environment for future generations.
  • Comprehensive Service: We offer a full spectrum of services from planning to maintenance, ensuring the success of every project.
  • Commitment to Quality: Quality is at the core of everything we do, exceeding client expectations with top-notch materials and craftsmanship.
  • Client-Centric Approach: We build lasting relationships through trust, transparency, and a relentless focus on meeting client needs.
  • Project Management: We ensure prudent decision-making and maintain transparency in project controls and reporting, optimizing industry best practices.
